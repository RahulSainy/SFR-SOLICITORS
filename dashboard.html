Medical Expert Witness Request Consent Form

As a solicitor, I acknowledge and agree that:

  • The medical expert witness I am requesting is being retained to provide an independent opinion in the form of an expert report, which will be provided to me for the purpose of assisting me in the preparation and presentation of my client's case.
  • The medical expert witness is not my client and owes no duty of care to me or my client.
  • The medical expert witness is not required to advocate for me or my client, and may have views that differ from mine or my client's.
  • The medical expert witness has been provided with a copy of this consent form and has agreed to abide by its terms.

By submitting this request for a medical expert witness, I confirm that I have read and understood the above terms, and agree to be bound by them.
