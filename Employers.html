Industrial and work related injuries

Industrial and work related injuries can be a difficult problem to manage. Resolve Medicolegal can provide Experts who have provided pursuer and defender reports for many years on common conditions such as HAVS, carpal tunnel syndrome, RSI and back pain.

If you would like to discuss such cases, please contact us. We will put you in touch with a medical expert who can offer advice, prior to a request of a report or examination.

Resolve Medicolegal can also coordinate investigations and treatments for your staff, to expedite return to work. Please contact us and we can provide you with estimation of costs.

Referrals are then made securely online, either to individual experts or for the first available appointment within a particular speciality.
