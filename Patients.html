Compensation claims

As an individual, making a claim for compensation can be a challenging process. Resolve Medicolegal can provide an Expert report and arrange the necessary private investigations. We can also arrange the appropriate treatment to assist you in your recovery.

Please contact our office, where our experienced administration team can offer advice and further guidance.

It should be noted, that Resolve Medicolegal is not directly involved with organising personal injury claims. Any queries should initially be made through your solicitor.

Referrals are then made securely online, either to individual experts or for the first available appointment within a particular speciality.
